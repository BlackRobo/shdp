Preventative Dental Care

We offer a range of services including examinations, hygiene and fissure sealants. Regular dental checkups are essential to maintaining oral health and pre-empting potential problems before they magnify.

Restorative Dental Surgery

When problems are identified, we offer all the usual general dentistry to resolve issue such as fillings, root canals and extraction to more specialised techniques such as periodontal treatment.

Cosmetic Dental Treatment

In addition to offering crowns, veneers and dental implants, Stratford House Dental Practice also offer a full range of cosmetic treatments to improve your smile. In addition we can also offer treatments to improve the appearance of your facial skin – the frame to your smile.

Cavity Treatment

If you have tooth decay, then you will need to have a filling. The area must be prepared prior to a filling, by removing all the decay and shaping the cavity to hold the filling in place. If the decay is more extensive, your dentist may need to place an inlay or only (partial crown).
